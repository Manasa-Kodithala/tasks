Beauty of Hyderabad

Hyderabad, city, Telangana state, south-central India. It is Telangana 's largest and most-populous city and is the major urban centre for all of south-central interior India. The place has created such a kind of influence on its culture and people that one can easily identify a hyderabadi from a large group of people.
